About me

I'm a bachelor's graduate in Communication Systems from Ecole Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), currently working as a research intern at the VGI Lab, in Seoul National University (SNU) .

My research interests lie in Computer Vision, particularly semantic segmentation and, more recently, Vision-Language Models (VLMs). I'm always open to collaborating with people who share similar passions.
